About TopCog

One person. A lot of games.

TopCog is an indie game studio founded and run by Matthew Miskiewicz. He handles everything — design, programming, art direction, publishing, and community.

Matt holds B.S. degrees in Computer Engineering and Electrical Engineering, plus a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering. He's been programming for over 15 years and has been running TopCog since 2014.

TopCog specializes in idle RPGs, incremental games, and action roguelites — games with deep systems, satisfying progression loops, and insane replayability. From the original Idle Quest to the latest release Neon Spellstorm, every game is built with a love for the genre and a commitment to quality.
